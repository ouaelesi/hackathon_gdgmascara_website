About us

We are a group of students who wants to take
e-learning to the next level:
Aztics is a team of creative engineering students
who believe that the best way to learn is to find
the right person to guide you and answer your
questions rather than spending too much time
on following classic courses without making real
progress. Our mission is to help students and the
community to be more productive and motivated
about exchanging informations, and to give the
chance for professionals to share their knowledge
with the learners.
By simplifying the learning process and making
it more interactive, e-learning has never
been this fun before.
